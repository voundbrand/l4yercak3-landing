## Deploy These for Clients — Don't Build From Scratch

Each template below is a complete funnel structure: landing page, thank-you page, email sequence, and CRM pipeline. Customize copy and branding per client — the structure stays the same.

---

**1. Home Services (Plumber, HVAC, Electrician)**

- Landing page: emergency + standard service split
- Instant quote request form (service type, urgency, address)
- Automated text/WhatsApp confirmation with ETA
- 3-email follow-up: confirmation, day-before reminder, post-service review request

*Conversion hook: "Get a quote in 60 seconds" — speed beats everything in emergency services.*

---

**2. Salon & Beauty**

- Landing page: services menu with online booking
- Self-service appointment scheduler (service, stylist, time)
- Automated confirmation + 24h reminder + "running late?" text
- Re-booking sequence 3 weeks after appointment

*Conversion hook: "Book your next appointment in 30 seconds" — eliminates the back-and-forth texting.*

---

**3. Restaurant & Hospitality**

- Landing page: menu highlights, ambiance photos, reservation CTA
- Online reservation form (date, time, party size, dietary needs)
- Automated confirmation + day-of reminder
- Post-visit sequence: review request + "book your next visit" offer

*Conversion hook: "Reserve your table — instant confirmation" — no waiting for a callback.*

---

**4. Dental & Medical Practice**

- Landing page: services, insurance accepted, new patient CTA
- New patient intake form (multi-step: personal info, insurance, medical history)
- Appointment scheduling with provider selection
- Reminder sequence: 1 week, 1 day, 1 hour before appointment

*Conversion hook: "New patients welcome — book online in 2 minutes" — removes the phone anxiety barrier.*

---

**5. Real Estate**

- Landing page: featured listings with search/filter
- Inquiry form (buying/selling, budget range, location, timeline)
- Automated property match notifications
- Viewing scheduling with calendar integration

*Conversion hook: "Get matched with properties in your budget — instantly" — captures leads 24/7 even on weekends.*

---

**6. Coaching & Consulting**

- Landing page: authority content, testimonials, discovery call CTA
- Application form (business size, revenue, main challenge, budget)
- Automated qualification email based on responses
- Calendar booking for qualified leads only

*Conversion hook: "Apply for a free strategy session" — positions the coach as selective, not desperate.*
