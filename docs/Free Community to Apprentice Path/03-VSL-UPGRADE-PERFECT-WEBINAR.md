# VSL: Premium/VIP Upgrade (Perfect Webinar Style)

**Location:** First video in Classroom (Module 1, Lesson 1)
**Length:** 2-3 minutes
**Purpose:** Upgrade free members to €29 Premium or €49 VIP

---

## Perfect Webinar Structure

1. **Hook** (15 sec) - Big promise, pattern interrupt
2. **Story** (45 sec) - Your journey, credibility
3. **Content** (60 sec) - The 3 secrets/beliefs they need to change
4. **Stack** (30 sec) - What they get at Premium/VIP
5. **Close** (30 sec) - CTA, urgency

---

## Script

```
[HOOK]

Most builders stay broke not because they can't build - but because they never learn the three things that actually make money. In the next two minutes, I'm going to tell you what they are. And if you want to shortcut the whole process, I'll show you how.

[STORY]

I spent 20 years building software. Enterprise systems, real products, stuff businesses depend on. But when I tried to go solo, I couldn't sell any of it.

So I stopped coding for five years and learned sales and marketing. Cold outreach, positioning, pricing - the stuff that turns projects into paychecks.

And I realized the gap isn't code. It's everything else.

[CONTENT - 3 Secrets]

Here's what I learned:

Secret one: You don't need a better idea. You need a better offer. Most builders obsess over features when clients buy outcomes.

Secret two: You don't need more leads. You need a system. Random outreach doesn't work. Consistent outreach with the right message does.

Secret three: You don't need permission. You need reps. Stop waiting to feel ready. Start shipping and fix it live.

These three things changed everything for me. And they're what separate builders who make money from builders who just make projects.

[STACK]

Now, I put everything I know about this into two tiers inside this community.

Premium is €29 a month. You get the full template library - outreach scripts, pricing frameworks, scoping tools. The stuff that took me years to figure out.

VIP is €49 a month. You get everything in Premium plus live access to me. Office hours twice a month. Monthly group calls. Bring your project, get real feedback.

[CLOSE]

If you're serious about making money from what you build - not just building for the sake of it - pick your tier and let's go.

Links are below this video.

And if you want to skip the line entirely - work on real client projects with me and get paid - check out the Apprenticeship module. That's for people who are ready to stop practicing and start shipping.

See you inside.
```
