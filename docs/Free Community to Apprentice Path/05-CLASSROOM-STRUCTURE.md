# Classroom Structure

## Overview

The classroom is designed to:
1. Deliver value immediately
2. Upsell Premium/VIP
3. Seed the Apprenticeship for those who are ready

The first video everyone sees is the upgrade pitch (Perfect Webinar style).

---

| Lesson | Title | Type | Access |
|--------|-------|------|--------|

- This is the Perfect Webinar upgrade video
- See `03-VSL-UPGRADE-PERFECT-WEBINAR.md`

## Module 2: The Fundamentals

**Purpose:** Core education for free members

| Lesson | Title | Type | Access |
|--------|-------|------|--------|
| 1 | Why Most Builders Fail | Video (5-7 min) | Free |
| 2 | The 3 Things You Need | Video (5-7 min) | Free |
| 3 | How to Pick a Project Worth Finishing | Video (5-7 min) | Free |
| 4 | How to Build an Offer | Video (5-7 min) | Free |
| 5 | How to Reach Buyers | Video (5-7 min) | Free |

### Lesson 1: Why Most Builders Fail
- The finishing gap
- The selling gap
- Why tutorials don't translate to income
- The real difference between hobbyists and professionals

### Lesson 2: The 3 Things You Need
- A project worth finishing
- An offer (not just a product)
- A way to reach buyers (system, not tactics)

### Lesson 3: How to Pick a Project Worth Finishing
- Market signals vs personal interest
- Scope test (useful v1 in 2 weeks)
- The money path
- When to kill a project

### Lesson 4: How to Build an Offer
- Product vs offer distinction
- The 5 parts: outcome, price, timeline, scope, risk reversal
- Offer template
- Common pricing mistakes

### Lesson 5: How to Reach Buyers
- Why "build in public" isn't a sales strategy
- The system: WHO (ICP) + WHERE + HOW
- Cold vs warm outreach
- The numbers game

### Lesson 1: What is the Apprenticeship?

- See `04-VSL-APPRENTICESHIP.md`

### Lesson 2: How to Know If You're Ready
Checklist:
- [ ] You can already build (or you're close)
- [ ] You've shipped something, even if small
- [ ] You can commit 10-20+ hours/week
- [ ] You're tired of learning without earning
- [ ] You have €5K to invest
- [ ] You don't need constant hand-holding

### Lesson 3: Apply Here
- Link to application form on website
- Brief reminder of what they're applying for
