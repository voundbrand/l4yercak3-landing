# YouTube Content Strategy

## Purpose

YouTube drives traffic to the free Skool community. Every video should:
1. Provide standalone value
2. Position you as the expert
3. CTA to join the community

---

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Vibe Coding / AI Building (40%)
- Tutorials on building with AI
- Tool breakdowns (Cursor, v0, Claude, etc.)
- "Build with me" sessions
- Project walkthroughs

### Pillar 2: Shipping & Selling (40%)
- How to finish projects
- Finding customers
- Pricing strategies
- Sales conversations
- Client management

### Pillar 3: Behind the Scenes (20%)
- Your projects and journey
- Client work stories (anonymized)
- Wins and failures
- Community highlights

---

## Video Types

### Type A: Tutorial (10-20 min)
**Purpose:** SEO, discovery, establish expertise
**Example Titles:**
- "Build a Lead Gen System with AI in 30 Minutes"
- "How I Use Claude to Scope Client Projects"
- "Reddit Scraping for Customer Research (Full Walkthrough)"

**CTA:** "Want the template? Join the free community - link in description."

---

### Type B: Framework/Strategy (8-15 min)
**Purpose:** Thought leadership, shareability
**Example Titles:**
- "Why Your AI Project Won't Make Money (And How to Fix It)"
- "The 3 Questions Before You Build Anything"
- "How to Price Your Automation Services"

**CTA:** "I break this down more in the community. Join free - link below."

---

### Type C: Story/Case Study (5-12 min)
**Purpose:** Connection, proof, entertainment
**Example Titles:**

---

**Purpose:** Discovery, algorithm growth
**Example Topics:**
- Quick tips
- Hot takes
- Tool recommendations
- Common mistakes

**CTA:** "Full breakdown on my channel / Join the community - link in bio"

## Thumbnail Guidelines

- Face showing emotion (surprised, skeptical, confident)
- Bold text (3-5 words max)
- High contrast colors
- Consistent style/branding
- No clutter

---

## Title Formulas

- "How I [Result] in [Timeframe]"
- "The [Number] [Things] You Need to [Result]"
- "Why [Common Belief] is Wrong"
- "[Tool/Topic] for [Audience] (Complete Guide)"
- "I [Did Something] So You Don't Have To"
- "Stop [Mistake]. Do [Better Thing] Instead."

## Metrics to Track

- Views (discovery)
- Watch time / retention (content quality)
- Comments (engagement)
- Community joins (conversion)
- Subscriber growth (long-term)

**Primary KPI:** Community joins per video

---

## Notes

- First 30 seconds matter most - hook hard
- Deliver value before asking for anything
- Be yourself - authenticity > polish
- Consistency beats virality
- Every video should make someone want to join the community
