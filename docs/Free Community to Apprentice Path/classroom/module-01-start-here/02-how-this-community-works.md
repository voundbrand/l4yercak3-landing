### The Level System

Your level increases as you participate. Each level unlocks new content:

| Level | What Unlocks |
|-------|--------------|
| Level 1 | Full access to Free content |
| Level 2 | ICP Research Worksheet, Basic Outreach Script |
| Level 3 | Full ICP Deep Dive Template, Cold DM Templates, Email Sequences |
| Level 4 | Project Scoping Template, Pricing Framework, Proposal Template |
| Level 5 | Build Challenges access |

**How to level up:**
- Post in the community
- Reply to others
- Complete lessons
- Participate in challenges
