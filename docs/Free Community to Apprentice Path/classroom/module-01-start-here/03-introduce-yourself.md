## Lesson Content

### Your Turn

Before you dive into the training, take 2 minutes to introduce yourself.

**Go to the Welcome Thread:** [Link to Pinned Community Post]

---

### What to Share

Answer these three questions:

1. **What are you building?**
   - Current project, side hustle, or business idea
   - If you're not sure yet, that's fine - say so

2. **What's your biggest challenge right now?**
   - Finishing projects?
   - Finding clients?
   - Pricing your work?
   - Something else?

3. **What do you want to achieve in the next 90 days?**
   - Be specific
   - Money goal, project goal, or skill goal

---

### Why This Matters

- You'll get more relevant help when people know your situation
- Introductions unlock connection - others are working on similar things
- Writing it down creates accountability

---

### Example Introduction

> Hey everyone, I'm [Name].
>
> I'm building a lead generation tool for real estate agents. Got the MVP working but haven't launched it yet.
>
> Biggest challenge: I don't know how to price it or how to find my first paying customers.
>
> Goal for the next 90 days: Get 5 paying users and validate the idea before building more features.
>
> Looking forward to learning from everyone here.
