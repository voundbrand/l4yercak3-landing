| Element | Your Answer |

## PART 1: THE INTRODUCTION (5 minutes)

### Title Slide
- **Webinar Title:** [CURIOSITY-BASED HEADLINE]
- **Subtitle:** How to [ACHIEVE DESIRED RESULT] by [YOUR UNIQUE METHOD]

### Welcome Script

> "Welcome everyone to today's webinar!
>
> I'm [YOUR NAME], and I'm thrilled to share with you a transformative framework that has [YOUR CREDIBILITY/RESULT].
>
> **Stay till the end** because I have an exclusive opportunity for you to [PREVIEW OF OFFER]."

### Set Expectations
- What they'll learn (the "secrets")
- Why this is different from everything else they've tried
- What's possible for them

---

## PART 2: STORY 1 - THE ORIGIN STORY (15 minutes)

*Purpose: Share your journey from where THEY are to where THEY want to be*

### The Framework for Teaching Your Origin Story:

**Step 1: Story (Learned or Earned)**
> Tell HOW you discovered this framework

**Step 2: Strategy (What)**
> Introduce the high-level framework/approach

**Step 3: Tactics (How)**
> Walk through the steps (but don't give everything away - that's what they're buying)

**Step 4: Social Proof/Examples**
> Share case studies of others who've used this

---

**[SLIDE: My Journey Begins]**
> "A few years ago, I was just like many of you - [DESCRIBE THEIR CURRENT SITUATION THAT YOU ONCE EXPERIENCED]."

**[SLIDE: The Struggle]**
Challenges I faced:
- [CHALLENGE 1 - something they relate to]
- [CHALLENGE 2 - something they relate to]
- [CHALLENGE 3 - something they relate to]

**[SLIDE: The Realization]**
> "As I gained more experience, it became clear that [KEY INSIGHT THAT CHANGED EVERYTHING]."

**[SLIDE: The Aha Moment]**
> "Then I discovered [YOUR NEW OPPORTUNITY/METHOD]. I realized that [KEY REALIZATION ABOUT WHY OLD WAYS DON'T WORK]."

**[SLIDE: Embracing the New Way]**
> "I decided to [ACTION YOU TOOK] and the results were [DESCRIBE TRANSFORMATION]."

## PART 4: STORY 3 - INTERNAL BELIEF STORY (15 minutes)

*Purpose: Break through a false belief about THEMSELVES that's holding them back*

### Common Internal False Beliefs to Address:
- "I'm not smart/talented enough"
- "I don't have enough time"
- "I'm not tech-savvy"
- "I'm too old/young"
- "I don't have the right background"

### Internal Belief Story Script Outline:

**[SLIDE: The False Belief I Had]**
> "I used to think that [INTERNAL FALSE BELIEF - something they also believe about themselves]."

**[SLIDE: The Downside of This Belief]**
> "This belief led to [NEGATIVE CONSEQUENCES]."

**[SLIDE: The Shift in Mindset]**
> "What I realized was [NEW EMPOWERING BELIEF]."

**[SLIDE: The Power of This New Belief]**
> "Once I changed this belief, [POSITIVE RESULTS]."

**[SLIDE: Your Internal Transformation]**
> "I want you to consider: What internal beliefs might be holding YOU back?
>
> What if [REFRAME THEIR FALSE BELIEF]?"

## PART 5: STORY 4 - EXTERNAL BELIEF STORY (15 minutes)

*Purpose: Help them see through false beliefs about EXTERNAL barriers*

### Common External False Beliefs to Address:

- "This won't work in my situation"

### External Belief Story Script Outline:

**[SLIDE: Shattering the External Belief]**

> The same is possible for you."

## QUICK REFERENCE: THE 4 STORIES

1. **Origin Story** (15 min) - Your journey from their situation to success
2. **Vehicle Story** (15 min) - How your NEW OPPORTUNITY works
3. **Internal Belief Story** (15 min) - Breaking false beliefs about THEMSELVES
4. **External Belief Story** (15 min) - Breaking false beliefs about CIRCUMSTANCES

**Remember:** Don't share the tactics (HOW) - that's what they're buying. Share the strategy (WHAT) and the stories (WHY).
