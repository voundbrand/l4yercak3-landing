# ICP Research Worksheet (Basic)

**Access:** Level 2 (Free)

---

## What This Is

Your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) defines exactly who you're building for. Without this, you're guessing.

---

## The Worksheet

### 1. Who are they?

**Job title / role:**
_Example: Marketing manager at a B2B SaaS company_

**Company size:**
_Example: 10-50 employees_

**Industry:**
_Example: Software, agencies, e-commerce_

---

### 2. What problem do they have?

**The pain (in their words):**
_Example: "I spend 5 hours a week manually compiling reports from different tools"_

**What it costs them:**
_Example: Time, missed insights, looks bad to leadership_

---

### 3. What have they tried?

**Previous solutions:**
_Example: Spreadsheets, hired a VA, tried Zapier but too complicated_

**Why those failed:**
_Example: Still manual, too expensive, too technical_

---

### 4. Where do they hang out?

**Online communities:**
_Example: LinkedIn, specific Slack groups, Reddit r/marketing_

**Publications / podcasts:**
_Example: Marketing Brew, specific industry newsletters_

---

### 5. One sentence ICP

Fill in the blanks:

**I help [TYPE OF PERSON] who [SPECIFIC SITUATION] to [OUTCOME].**

_Example: I help marketing managers at B2B SaaS companies who waste hours on manual reporting to get automated dashboards in 48 hours._

---

## Next Steps

1. Fill this out for ONE specific customer type
2. Find 3-5 places where these people hang out online
3. Go listen to how they talk about their problems
4. Update this worksheet with their actual language
